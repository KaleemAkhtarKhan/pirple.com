Delicious Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian pasta dish loved by many around the world. This hearty meal combines perfectly cooked spaghetti with a rich and flavorful meat sauce. What makes it so special is the combination of tender ground beef, aromatic herbs, and savory tomatoes. Plus, who can resist the generous sprinkle of Parmesan cheese on top?

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound (450g) spaghetti
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving
