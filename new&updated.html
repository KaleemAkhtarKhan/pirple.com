Delicious Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

About Spaghetti Bolognese

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian pasta dish that's hearty, flavorful, and loved by many around the world. It features perfectly cooked spaghetti noodles smothered in a rich, savory meat sauce, typically made with ground beef, tomatoes, onions, and aromatic herbs.

What it Looks Like:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound (450g) spaghetti
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving
